Welcome to my blog

I started this blog mid december 2014. It mostly consists of blog entries I wrote during Phase 0 of Dev Bootcamp. Those blog entries, which were usually written in under an hour, document my progression and understanding of new things presented to us. Go to the About Me section to read a brief summary of my life and how it led me to becoming a developer.
