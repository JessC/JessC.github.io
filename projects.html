    Hitch

    A ride sharing appwhre users are either drivers or passengers. Drivers post trips that they are plannign to take and how many seats are available. Passengers can view a list of upcoming trips or use a search function to find one. They can drag and drop their profile picture into a picture of a car to automatically be added to the trip. Drivers and passengers can communicate through an in app messaging system. At the end of the trip, a "star rating" voting system becomes availble to leave feedback on other members.
    Technologies:
  • * The front and back end are coupled and were built in Rails.
  • * Devise for authentication
  • * HTML5
  • * CSS3
  • * jQuery
  • * JavaScript
  • * AJAX
